The above image, built with Wordle, shows the most common words that occur in a collection of cocktail recipes. I had roughly a hundred recipes.

I've removed the words oz, 1/2 (and other fractions) and glass, which were the most common words. Unfortunately they were so common that they completely dominated the wordle. And there's not much insight in the fact that you drink cocktails from a glass.

As I expected, ice is the most common word followed closely by juice. I'm surprised by the relationship between vodka and gin, though.
